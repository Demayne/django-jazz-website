Jazzy Disciples

Jazzy Disciples is a vibrant and soulful jazz band hailing from the beautiful landscapes of Lesotho, Africa. With their unique blend of Motown-inspired melodies and African rhythms, they bring a fresh and energetic sound to the world of jazz music. Formed by a group of talented musicians passionate about spreading the joy of music, Jazzy Disciples captivate audiences with their smooth tunes and infectious rhythms.
